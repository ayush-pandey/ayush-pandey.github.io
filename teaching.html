Personalized Projects

In my introductory computer programming courses, I have students work on personalized projects that align with their interests, passions, and hobbies. These projects are designed to be open-ended and allow students to explore their interests while applying the programming concepts they learn in class. You can read more about the paper on this here and search for all projects that students have worked on in my classes: Search for student-proposed projects.
